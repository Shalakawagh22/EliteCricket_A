Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling

What age groups do you accept? ▾
We accept students from age 8 to 25. Our beginner program is ideal for younger students, while the advanced and elite programs cater to older, more experienced players.
Do I need prior cricket experience? ▾
Not at all! Our Foundation (Beginner) program is designed for complete beginners. Our coaches will assess your current level and place you in the right batch.
What equipment do I need to bring? ▾
For beginners, just comfortable sportswear and shoes. We provide basic equipment. Intermediate and advanced students are encouraged to have their own bat and pads. Check our store for gear.
Can I switch programs later? ▾
Absolutely. Our coaches regularly assess student progress and recommend program upgrades. You can move up at any time based on your development.
Is there a trial session available? ▾
Yes! We offer a free trial session for new students. Contact us or fill out the enrollment form and mention you'd like a trial session.
